Wind Up Memories

2012

Wind Up Memories | (12:03)

Role: Animation Director

The tales are being kept and piled up in the corners where they don’t hinder, along with the boxes and old items that have been kept in the attic as the years go by. From the forgotten stories of grandparents , the rhizome like narratives , their memories and what they have forgot, arises the animation short film Wind up memories. The story of a little girl that looks for her grandpa´s memories within the train and machines that lie in the dusty attic where the old things that no longer work are being kept .

This film was developed with the help of the “Fund for Cinematographic Development - Cultural Ministry of Colombia”
