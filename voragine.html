Voragine 31

Role: Guest Director

Dreading the present, thirty-one animators entered the jungle of their imagination to honor José Eustasio Rivera’s Novel, The Vortex, published in 1924. Musicians, producers, and composers joined in this cacophonous journey, immersed in the gloom of The Vortex’s legacy to create the works you see.

Vorágine 31, directed by the collective team of Animation Directors of ASIFA- Colombia Chapter.
